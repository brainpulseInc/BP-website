About

The firm

Brainpulse is a boutique consultancy with results at the core of everything we do. Our partners as well as consultants are former services, and digital practitioners with hands-on experience in businesses large to small, pureplay to omnichannel. We understand the challenges you face on a daily basis and have delivered greatness to a multitude of businesses craving a real edge.

Principles

Success is a moving target, which is why we’ve thrown out the cookie cutters. Instead, we focus on understanding your uniqueness, then consolidating it to your lasting advantage. Here’s our approach:

  • Client comes first

    We measure our success by our clients’ success. We identify your real needs and evolve your vision, not just deliver great work

  • Real results

    We create value for our clients that returns many times their investment. Our work must lead to you doing business in more effective and profitable ways.

  • Partnership

    We take a long-term view in our relationships. We appreciate our clients’ knowledge and experience and feel privileged to contribute to their achievements.

  • Strategy matters

    We will get you that quick win, as well as the edge for superior and sustained results. We always consider the business as a whole, the competitive system, and its evolving dynamics.

Reda Magani

Moving people and projects in the right direction.

What do a gaming giant, a digital agency and the California Academy of Sciences have in common? Each has become a success story in the case study roster of digital business guru Reda Magani.

With razor sharp focus and long-term vision, Reda has leverages his program, project, and portfolio management skills in order to transform roadblocks into opportunities. Over the past 15+ years, this has included boosting profitability by reengineering production operations, leading the PMO and an encompassing Digital Assets Management Program Implementation at the California Academy of Sciences, and running the Global PMO department at gaming giant Ubisoft, with teams located in North America, Europe and Asia. To name a few.

Successfully driving change while building star teams is Reda’s calling card. “Working directly with the digital teams to unearth the smallest clues towards better efficiency and profitability, this is what drives me. The more complex the better. Having worked all over the world, it is especially interesting to shed light on cultural differences and find ways to use the differences in a beneficial way.”

Reda is not one to sit on his laurels in the ever-changing digital landscape. Anticipating and acquiring the next core skill in the market and using this skill to improve corporate strategy. What’s more, his experience in risk management, comprehensive mitigation programs, and operational improvements in complex environments make him the go-to consultant in the digital domain.

With two feet firmly entrenched in the consulting world, Reda has witnessed what business people, especially technology entrepreneurs, endure to find professional success and balance in life. This is how Reda discovered his second calling as a life coach for technology entrepreneurs.

Whether for digital transformation or life coaching, Reda is driven to unlock insight, create concrete and sustainable strategies, and achieve long-lasting results.
